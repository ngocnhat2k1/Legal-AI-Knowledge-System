# Project Context

This file is the durable project brief for AI agents. Keep it current so a new agent can quickly understand what this project is, why it exists, who it serves, and what must stay out of scope.

For broad navigation, start from [Agent Memory Index](index.md). Keep this project context focused on the durable project brief, and link detailed feature, module, concept, workflow, decision, or review notes from the index as they become important.

Every factual claim below carries its verification date and source. Claims the research could not verify are quarantined in [Unverified / Do Not Rely On](#unverified--do-not-rely-on) and must not be restated as fact anywhere else in the repo.

## Summary

**Customs Assistant** is an internal web tool for the customs-declaration staff of a Vietnamese logistics company. v1 does exactly two things: (1) **exact tariff lookup**, deterministic, keyed by HS code + schedule + date, with no LLM anywhere near the numbers; and (2) **HS code candidate suggestion**, returning top-3 candidates plus verbatim legal-note evidence so a human decides. RAG over Vietnamese logistics law is a later phase, not v1.

**The pivot that defines this project.** The owner's original roadmap described a "legal reading assistant" — RAG plus a knowledge graph over statute prose. But the owner's actual daily job is customs declaration: look up an HS code, find the duty rate for this origin under this schedule, check whether a permit or specialized inspection applies. That is **table data with exact-key lookup**, not prose semantic search. The two products share infrastructure (a bitemporal document store, a Công báo ingestion pipeline, a citation model) but share **no core**. Semantic retrieval is the wrong tool for a duty rate: a rate is either the legally correct one for that (HS, schedule, date) tuple or it is a wrong answer on a legally binding declaration. **Customs first, law later.**

**Why the discipline matters, in one number.** In verification, a naive parse of the MFN tariff decree reported **94% success and was confidently wrong**: 1,520 HS codes appear in both Phụ lục I (export schedule) and Phụ lục II (import schedule), and 1,329 of them carry different rates. A parser blind to annex boundaries returns the *export* rate for an *import* question — silently, with no error, at 94% apparent success (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12, adversarial verification of ND 26/2023 gazette parts from congbao.chinhphu.vn). This project's characteristic failure is not missing data. It is **plausible-looking wrong data that reports success**.

## Product Purpose

Cut the time and error rate of the daily declaration loop, and produce a defensible paper trail — without ever becoming the thing that states a legally binding number on its own authority.

Three verified facts set the purpose:

1. **Wrong HS codes are expensive and the liability is the declarant's, never the tool's.** Under Nghị định 128/2020/NĐ-CP (as amended by 102/2021/NĐ-CP): wrong HS with no tax effect = 1–2 triệu VND (Điều 8 khoản 1); wrong HS causing underpayment, self-corrected in the Điều 9 khoản 2 windows = **10% of the shortfall**; found by customs = **20%** (Điều 9 khoản 3); characterized as trốn thuế = **1× to 3×** the evaded tax plus possible criminal referral (Điều 14). All carry truy thu of the full shortfall plus late-payment interest at **0.03%/day**. Administrative penalty limitation is 5 years; **tax and interest are recoverable for 10 years** from discovery (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://hethongphapluat.com/nghi-dinh-128-2020-nd-cp-quy-dinh-ve-xu-phat-vi-pham-hanh-chinh-trong-linh-vuc-hai-quan/dieu-8 and .../dieu-9).
2. **The problem is real and widespread.** 76% of enterprises report obstacles confirming HS codes, up from 66.3% in 2018 (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://diendandoanhnghiep.vn/ganh-nang-ma-hs-trach-nhiem-cua-co-quan-hai-quan-o-dau-10077631.html). The dairy case is the archetype: 8 producers including Vinamilk reportedly faced retroactive truy thu of **~700 tỷ VND** after customs reclassified Anhydrous Milk Fat from 0405.90.10 to 0405.90.90 in Dec 2014, destroying AANZFTA 0% eligibility, applied back to 2010 declarations (source: research 09 §2 — **no primary URL; illustrative, not established fact**; see [Business Rules → Unverified](business-rules.md#unverified--do-not-rely-on)).
3. **The only output contract that works is candidates + evidence for a human.** See [Core Workflows](#core-workflows) for the measured accuracy gap that forces this.

## Primary Users

5–50 internal staff at one Vietnamese logistics company — declaration officers and their supervisors. They are **domain experts, not laypeople**. The tool's job is to save an expert time and surface the evidence they would otherwise hunt for manually; it is not to replace their judgment, and it must never present itself as more certain than they are.

Consequences of this user profile:
- No public/customer surface in v1. No anonymous access.
- The audience can read Vietnamese legal text verbatim. **Quote the legal note; do not paraphrase it.** Paraphrase destroys the artifact's value as hồ sơ when a Chi cục Hải quan khu vực challenges the code.
- Known, identified users means an internal login is sufficient — and is strictly simpler than any chat-platform identity. See [Out of Scope](#out-of-scope) on Zalo.

## Operating Context

- **The legal ground moves faster than any crawl schedule.** Nghị định 72/2026/NĐ-CP was signed 09/03/2026 and took effect **the same day** ("kể từ ngày ký"), cutting petrol/naphtha/reformate MFN from 10% to 0%. It was published in Công báo số 157 on **24/03/2026 — 15 days after it was already binding law**. And it was valid **only until 30/04/2026**, a 52-day window, after which rates revert to ND 26/2023 (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12, live fetches of congbao.chinhphu.vn). There is a multi-week window in which the legally-in-force rate exists in machine-readable form **nowhere** — only as a 200-DPI scan. A "scrape the latest version" design would serve 0% petrol forever.
- **A decree can be issued, suspended, and extended within 10 weeks.** ND 46/2026/NĐ-CP (26/01/2026) was issued to replace the food-safety framework ND 15/2018; NQ 09/2026/NQ-CP (04/02/2026) **suspended it barely a week later**; the suspension was later extended until the amended Food Safety Law takes effect. ND 15/2018 therefore remains operative today (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?docid=216891&pageid=27160 and https://baochinhphu.vn/tiep-tuc-ap-dung-nghi-dinh-15-2018-nd-cp-ve-an-toan-thuc-pham-cho-den-khi-co-quy-dinh-moi-102260408123934123.htm). Every rule needs effective-from / effective-to / suspended-by / superseded-by — not just a citation.
- **Institutions renamed under the agent's feet.** Since 01/03/2025 **Tổng cục Hải quan no longer exists**; it is **Cục Hải quan** under Bộ Tài chính with 20 Chi cục Hải quan khu vực (Nghị định 29/2025/NĐ-CP, Quyết định 382/QĐ-BTC). Documents are now numbered `-CHQ`, not `-TCHQ` (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://xaydungchinhsach.chinhphu.vn/quyet-dinh-382-qd-btc-quy-dinh-chuc-nang-nhiem-vu-quyen-han-va-co-cau-to-chuc-cua-cuc-hai-quan-119250228165530471.htm). Likewise Bộ NN&PTNT + Bộ TN&MT → **Bộ Nông nghiệp và Môi trường** (`TT-BNNPTNT` → `TT-BNNMT`), and Bộ GTVT merged into **Bộ Xây dựng** eff. 01/03/2025 (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 03, 08). **Model ministries as entities with aliases and validity ranges.** A code table keyed on ministry name breaks here.
- **Two hard deadlines are visible.** Essentially the entire FTA decree corpus (the 2022 batch: 112–129/2022, plus VIFTA 131/2024 and CEPA 143/2026) **expires 31/12/2027**, colliding with the **AHTN/HS 2028** nomenclature switch effective **01/01/2028** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10; https://www.wcoomd.org/en/topics/nomenclature/instrument-and-tools/hs-nomenclature-2028-edition/amendments-effective-from-1-january-2028.aspx). Do not hardcode AHTN 2022. Do not model "the current rate" as a scalar.
- **VNACCS/VCIS has a shelf life.** Replacement by the "Hải quan số" system is targeted **31/12/2026**, with a revised Luật Hải quan expected before the National Assembly around 10/2026 (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://baophapluat.vn/hai-quan-tang-toc-chuyen-doi-so-thi-diem-mo-hinh-thong-quan-tap-trung-bai-1-tu-nen-tang-vnaccs-vcis-den-buoc-chuyen-moi-cua-hai-quan-so.html). Anything built against VNACCS message formats has roughly an 18-month shelf life.

## Core Workflows

**The daily declaration loop the tool sits inside** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 08): classify HS → check policy (cấm / giấy phép / điều kiện / kiểm tra chuyên ngành) → obtain licences and register inspection on VNSW → declare in ECUS middleware → receive **phân luồng** (green/yellow/red) → yellow = document check, red = physical inspection → pay duty → thông quan. v1 touches only the first two steps, and only as a research aid.

### Workflow 1 — Exact tariff lookup (deterministic)

Input: HS code + schedule (MFN or a named FTA) + a date. Output: the rate as published, **plus the decree it came from, plus the as-of date**, plus an explicit refusal when the snapshot may be stale.

Non-negotiable design facts:
- **No LLM in the path.** This is a keyed table read.
- **`(HS, country) → rate` is not even a function.** MFN vs FTA is conditional, not automatic: RCEP Điều 4 requires origin rules *plus a valid C/O*. "The tax is 0%" is wrong; "0% *if* you hold a valid C/O, else 15% MFN" is right. RCEP Điều 6.2 carries a highest-rate rule across annexes for certain multi-origin goods — verified verbatim in the text: *"Mức thuế suất cao nhất tại các Phụ lục Biểu thuế áp dụng cho cùng hàng hóa có xuất xứ từ các nước thành viên..."* (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12, ND 129/2022 gazette text).
- **Cells that are not rates.** `*` means the good is **excluded** from the FTA rate, not zero. TRQ goods (headings 04.07, 17.01, 24.01, 25.01, verified in ND 129/2022 text) depend on quota status, with out-of-quota rates in a different annex. Phụ lục III (used cars) carries **absolute/mixed duties in USD**, not percentages — a `%` regex finds zero rows there (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12).
- **Correct 2026 MFN is a consolidation, not a document.** ND 26/2023 is the base and is not superseded; the correct current rate is that base plus an amendment chain, and there is no official văn bản hợp nhất published as machine-readable data (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10). ⚠️ **The chain itself is an open question** — research 10 and research 12 give different, individually non-exhaustive lists. Establish it from Công báo before ingesting; see [Tariff System](concepts/tariff-system.md) and Open Question 6 in [Bootstrap Plan](planning/00-bootstrap.md).
- **Output contract:** cite the decree and the date; refuse when the snapshot may be stale. A research aid that shows its sources, never an answer engine that states a rate.

### Workflow 2 — HS candidate suggestion (top-3 + verbatim evidence)

Input: a product description. Output: 3 candidates, each citing the **specific** Chapter Note, Section Note, EN paragraph, SEN entry, or công văn that supports it, **quoted verbatim**. The human decides.

**The measured case for this contract — this is why the note exists.** On HSCodeComp (632 expert-annotated products, 10-digit target), human experts score **95.0%**; the best autonomous agent (SmolAgent + GPT-5 VLM) scores **46.8%**; GPT-5 with no tools scores **29.0%**. Accuracy collapses down the hierarchy: ~82% at 2-digit → 29–47% at 10-digit (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/html/2510.19631). Against that, the Korea Customs Service system — which predicts 6-digit subheadings and **retrieves the relevant key sentences from the HS manual as explainable evidence for each candidate** — reaches **top-3 accuracy 93.9%** across 925 difficult subheadings on 5,000 recent classification requests, with a 32-expert user study confirming reduced review time (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.10922). **The gap between 47% and 93.9% is not model capability. It is the output contract.**

Three further findings that should kill naive designs before they are built:
- **Test-time scaling does not help here.** Majority voting and self-reflection gave negligible gains on HSCodeComp — unlike other reasoning domains.
- **Prompt-stuffing human decision rules *decreased* performance** for most systems. More rules in the prompt is not better.
- **Errors are "Error but Valid."** The model emits a real, legitimate-looking HS code that is wrong. **There is no syntactic signal of failure** — no exception, no parse error, no red flag. It flows into VNACCS, is accepted, and surfaces three years later as a post-clearance audit (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/html/2510.19631).

Design directives that follow (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 09 synthesizing the above plus https://arxiv.org/html/2605.14857):
- **Structure the pipeline; don't let the LLM freelance.** A deterministic agentic workflow whose control flow is dictated by the tariff hierarchy — chapter → heading → subheading → national 8-digit as separate stages, with chapter/section notes **pre-compiled offline into typed inclusion/exclusion/priority clauses** — scored 4-digit 75.0% top-1 / 91.5% top-3 and 6-digit 64.2% top-1 / 78.3% top-3 on HSCodeComp, roughly **2× the best autonomous agent**. A 27B open-weight model reached 84.2% (4-digit) agreement with the frontier model — you do not need a frontier model for most of the pipeline.
- **Model GRI order as code, not as a prompt instruction.** GRI 1 → 2 → 3(a) → 3(b) → 3(c) → 4 → 5 → 6 is a decision procedure applied in strict sequential order; you may not jump to GRI 3 if GRI 1 resolves it.
- **Abstain loudly.** The highest-value output on a hard good is *"these two headings both plausibly apply, here are the competing notes, this needs xác định trước mã số."* Routing to the Điều 28 process is a **feature**, not a failure — it is the only mechanism producing legal certainty in Vietnam.
- **Never let the user's preferred code into the prompt as a premise.** *"If you want your HTS to be X (even if the correct HTS is Y), AI will give you an argument (or three) to support your preferred HTS"* (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://aomeara.com/why-ai-tools-for-tariff-classification-may-lead-you-down-the-wrong-road/). The model is a sycophantic advocate, not a judge — and a manufactured argument for a preferred code is precisely the paper trail for a trốn thuế characterization.
- **Even the ground truth is contestable.** An audit of 226 disagreements found **~42.5% of "wrong" model predictions were actually better supported by HS rules than the published ground truth** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/html/2605.14857). Optimizing for agreement with customs is the commercially correct target; it is not the same thing as being right. Say so in the UI.

## In Scope

- **v1 feature 1 — Exact tariff lookup.** Deterministic read keyed by HS + schedule + date. MFN plus the FTA schedules. Every answer cites its decree and as-of date, and refuses when stale.
- **v1 feature 2 — HS candidate suggestion.** Top-3 with verbatim legal-note evidence, structured pipeline, explicit abstention.
- **Bitemporal data model from day 1.** Valid time (when the law is in force) vs transaction time (when we learned it). Required by vacatio legis, retroactive provisions, delayed/conditional effectiveness, and đính chính/republication (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 02, citing https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00039). Point-in-time query is a deterministic interval predicate: `tv.valid_start ≤ t < coalesce(tv.valid_end, +∞)`.
- **Công báo as the ingestion source.** `congbao.chinhphu.vn/robots.txt` is `User-agent: * / Allow: /`; no Cloudflare, no JS required; documents ship as **DOCX and text-layer PDF**, and these are the `_signed`, legally authoritative versions (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 04 and 12).
- **Annex-aware parsing with a real Word parser** (LibreOffice → docx → `w:tbl/w:tr/w:tc`), **not `textutil`**. See [Unverified](#unverified--do-not-rely-on) — this is the one gap a builder must close before trusting anything.
- **Explicit modelling of non-rate states:** `*` exclusion, TRQ in/out of quota, absolute/mixed duty, C/O-conditional.
- **Later phases (named, not built in v1):** RAG over Vietnamese logistics law; quản lý chuyên ngành / permit checking; consistency auditing of the company's own historical declarations; drafting the hồ sơ xác định trước mã số.

## Out of Scope

Each exclusion below is a decision with a reason, not an omission.

- **Zalo — in any form.** Build a plain web app. For ≤50 known internal users, a verified OA on the free tier has **no OpenAPI and no chatbot**; the cheapest tier that runs a custom API-driven bot is **Tăng trưởng at 2.500.000đ/năm** (pricing effective 01/06/2026, verified 2026-07-17, source: https://zalo.solutions/oa/pricing). Messaging is metered for *customer* notification: **8 free tin tư vấn per 48h from the user's last interaction**, then 55đ/tin, with a 7-day OpenAPI sending window (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://oa.zalo.me/home/resources/news/thong-bao-chinh-sach-gui-tin-va-quy-dinh-phi-gui-tin_1433049880779375099). Your own staff would get rate-limited talking to your own tool. Zalo also gives an opaque `user_id`, not an employee identity — you'd build a mapping table anyway, at which point a web login is simpler. Unofficial libraries (zca-js, zlapi, zcago) breach ToS §4.7 and risk banning **an employee's personal Zalo account** — in Vietnam close to civic infrastructure. Disqualifying. **Revisit only if the roadmap ever requires facing drivers/customers outside the company.** (Note: reports 01 and 11 disagree on whether a separate OA-independent Zalo Bot API now exists; the decision does not depend on resolving that, since the reason to skip Zalo is fit and identity, not availability.)
- **Neo4j.** No graph database in v1. Tariff lookup is a keyed table read; there is no graph traversal in the v1 critical path. PostgreSQL models the amendment/reference graph adequately at this scale.
- **Qdrant.** No dedicated vector DB. v1's semantic surface is HS-note retrieval only; pgvector in the existing PostgreSQL is sufficient and removes an operational component.
- **MinIO.** No object store service. Source documents can live on disk/volume at v1 scale.
- **BullMQ.** No job queue. Ingestion is a scheduled batch, not an event stream, and the amendment cadence (see [Operating Context](#operating-context)) is measured in weeks.
- **Autonomous HS assignment.** Never. The best independently-verified autonomous number is ~47% at 10-digit vs 95% human, and the errors are "Error but Valid" — silent, delayed, and the declarant's liability. *"An AI tool that produces a plausible-looking ten-digit number is not reasonable care"* (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://internationaltradematters.com/discussion/ai-customs-compliance-for-smes/). "The platform told me the HS code" is not a defence.
- **Anti-dumping / safeguard duties (chống bán phá giá, tự vệ).** Excluded from v1 despite being, for the archetypal query "steel from China", the *most* important number — which is exactly why a half-built version is worse than none. They are country-specific, live in individual **Quyết định-BCT**, have **no consolidated machine-readable register**, and require per-case scraping of pvtm.gov.vn with sơ bộ → chính thức → rà soát cuối kỳ lifecycle tracking. The hardest dataset by far (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10; https://pvtm.gov.vn/).
- **TTĐB / BVMT per-HS mapping.** Excluded because **they are not HS-keyed in law**. Verified via the customs API itself: a TTĐB query returns rows like `"I. Hàng hóa"`, `"1. Thuốc lá điếu…"`, `"2. Rượu"` with **`MA_HS: None`**; BVMT returns `"I. Xăng, dầu, mỡ nhờn"` with no HS. The statutory tables are by product category, not HS line. Any per-HS mapping we build is **our own editorial inference and a liability surface** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10). BVMT is also volatile — NQ 19/2026/QH16 (12/04/2026) set petrol/jet fuel to 0 đ/lít for 16/4–30/6/2026, later extended to 30/9/2026 — changing every few months by resolution, not decree.

## Domain Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **HS** (Harmonized System) | WCO nomenclature: 21 Sections / 97 Chapters, **6 digits internationally harmonized**. Revised roughly every 5 years; **HS 2028 enters force 01/01/2028** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://www.wcoomd.org/en/topics/nomenclature/instrument-and-tools/hs-nomenclature-2028-edition/amendments-effective-from-1-january-2028.aspx). |
| **AHTN** | ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature — the regional extension of HS to **8 digits**. Vietnam's list is **Thông tư 31/2022/TT-BTC** (eff. 01/12/2022, built on HS 2022 / AHTN 2022, still operative in 2026): **21 phần · 97 chương · 1.228 nhóm (4-digit) · 4.084 phân nhóm (6-digit) · 11.414 dòng hàng (8-digit)** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10). |
| **GRI** (6 quy tắc tổng quát) | The six General Rules of Interpretation, applied **in strict sequential order**. GRI 1 = heading text + Section/Chapter Notes decide (titles are reference only); GRI 2 = incomplete/unassembled, mixtures; GRI 3 = (a) most specific → (b) essential character → (c) last in numerical order; GRI 4 = most akin; GRI 5 = cases/packing; GRI 6 = subheadings, same level only. Published as Phụ lục II of TT 31/2022 (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://www.wcoomd.org/-/media/wco/public/global/pdf/topics/nomenclature/instruments-and-tools/hs-interpretation-general-rules/0001_2012e_gir.pdf). **Binding authority = heading/subheading text + Section, Chapter and Subheading Notes.** WCO Explanatory Notes and the Compendium of Classification Opinions are authoritative/persuasive but **not binding**; ASEAN **SEN** (circulated in Vietnam by Công văn 3866/TCHQ-TXNK, 24/07/2023) is not independently binding and loses to the HS EN. |
| **MFN** (thuế suất ưu đãi / NK ưu đãi) | The default preferential import rate applied to WTO-member origins absent an FTA claim. Base decree: **Nghị định 26/2023/NĐ-CP** (31/05/2023, eff. 15/07/2023), Phụ lục II. Still the base decree — amended, never replaced (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?pageid=27160&docid=208020). |
| **FTA** (thuế suất ưu đãi đặc biệt) | Special preferential rates under a free trade agreement, each in its own Nghị định. 17 decrees were issued on 30/12/2022 covering 2022–2027 (CPTPP=115, EVFTA=116, UKVFTA=117, ATIGA=126, RCEP=129, …), plus **VIFTA (131/2024, Israel)** and **CEPA (143/2026, UAE)**. ⚠️ **128/2022 is not an FTA tariff decree** — do not assume a contiguous 112–129 range. **The whole corpus expires 31/12/2027** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10). An FTA rate is **conditional on origin rules + a valid C/O**, never automatic. |
| **C/O** (Chứng nhận xuất xứ) | Certificate of Origin — the document that unlocks an FTA rate. **QĐ 1103/QĐ-BCT (21/04/2025) revoked VCCI's authority** to issue C/O, CNM and REX codes; issuance is centralised under MOIT / Sở Công Thương. eCoSys migrated to **https://co.moit.gov.vn** at 12:00 on 22/12/2025. 14 preferential forms: D (ASEAN), E (China), AHK, AJ/VJ, AK/VK, AI, X (Cambodia), EUR.1 (EVFTA), EUR.1 UKVFTA, plus RCEP and CPTPP forms. **Any pre-2025 knowledge saying "VCCI issues form B/ICO" is wrong** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://logistics.gov.vn/tin-hoat-dong/bo-cong-thuong-thu-hoi-quyen-cap-c-o-cnm-va-ma-so-rex-tu-vcci-de-chuan-hoa-he-thong-cap-c-o-trong-boi-canh-moi). |
| **TRQ** (hạn ngạch thuế quan) | Tariff-rate quota. Rate depends on quota status; **out-of-quota rates live in a separate annex** (Phụ lục IV of ND 26/2023). Verified TRQ headings in the ND 129/2022 text: **04.07 (eggs), 17.01 (sugar), 24.01 (tobacco), 25.01 (salt)** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12). |
| **VBHN** (văn bản hợp nhất) | Consolidated text merging a base document with its amendments. **This changed in 2026 and it is load-bearing.** Pháp lệnh 01/2026/UBTVQH16 (issued 10/06/2026, eff. 01/07/2026) provides verbatim: *"Văn bản hợp nhất được cơ quan, tổ chức, cá nhân **sử dụng làm căn cứ chính thức** trong viện dẫn và áp dụng pháp luật"* — consolidated texts are **now an official basis for citing and applying law**, which they were not before (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://congbao.chinhphu.vn/van-ban/phap-lenh-so-01-2026-ubtvqh16-469837.htm). **Use published VBHN as the text layer; do not compute consolidation from amendment instructions** — those are natural-language and irregular ("bổ sung Điều 5a", "bãi bỏ cụm từ X tại Khoản 2"), and a text-mutation engine is a correctness liability with no authority to fall back on. Note: **no official VBHN exists for the tariff schedules as machine-readable data** — correct 2026 MFN must still be composed by hand (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10). |
| **Hiệu lực** | Legal force / effectivity status. vbpl.vn models it first-class with badge values `Còn hiệu lực` (CHL), `Hết hiệu lực một phần`, `Hết hiệu lực toàn bộ` (HHL), `Chưa có hiệu lực`, plus JSON fields `effFrom`, `effTo`, `status` (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 04, 07). Scale of the problem: in one published Vietnamese corpus of 1,703 SBV documents, **863 were fully repealed, 191 partially repealed, and only 639 effective — ~62% dead or partly dead law** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://lexuanbach.github.io/publication/ACIIDS2026a.pdf). Temporal validity must be a **hard filter, not a ranking signal**: LLMs exhibit a measured **recency bias — preferring newer provisions even when the older version applies — that naive RAG does not fix** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23497). |
| **Điều / Khoản / Điểm** | Article / clause / point — the structural units of a Vietnamese legal document (≈ German §/Absatz/Satz). The nesting is the retrieval substrate: measured on the German BGB (2,455 sections, 525 gold-labelled questions), **Recall@10 was 0.47 for subsection-level and 0.46 for section-level indexing, vs 0.31–0.37 for every fixed-size strategy** — structural chunking beats fixed-size decisively, and Điều vs Khoản as the *index* unit is statistically a wash (Friedman p < 0.0001, paired permutation with Holm correction). **What matters is not cutting across structural boundaries.** LLM-based chunking *lost* and lost badly on cost: Lumber 9h–11h41m and RAPTOR 3h–5h51m to build, vs **51 seconds** for section chunking, for worse recall (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.19806). Correct configuration: **index per-Khoản, return the whole Điều.** |
| **Xác định trước mã số** | Advance ruling on HS code, under **Luật Hải quan 2014 Điều 28**. File mẫu **01/XĐTMS/TXNK** plus technical dossier **at least 60 days before** shipment; processing 30 days (60 for complex cases); **validity max 3 years**. **It ceases to apply if the actual goods differ from the samples and documents submitted** — it protects the *described* good, not the *shipment*. Dossier rules were amended by **Thông tư 121/2025/TT-BTC** (issued 18/12/2025, **eff. 01/02/2026**) (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 09; https://www.pwc.com/vn/vn/publications/news-brief/251223-new-customs-procedures-effective-from-1-february-2026.html). **This is the only mechanism producing legal certainty — routing a user to it is a feature.** |
| **Quản lý chuyên ngành** | Specialized/sectoral management — the non-tariff requirement layer: hàng cấm, giấy phép, hàng có điều kiện, kiểm dịch, kiểm tra chất lượng, an toàn thực phẩm. Framework: **ND 69/2018/NĐ-CP** (still in force; replacement in draft). **There is no single master list** — each ministry publishes its own "bảng mã số HS" circular, and that is the join key. **Not everything keys on HS**: CITES keys on **species**; phế liệu keys on HS **+ a firm-level giấy phép môi trường**; the post-Luật 78/2025/QH15 quality regime keys on **risk tier** (thấp/trung bình/cao). An HS-only data model is structurally insufficient (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 08). |
| **VNACCS** (/VCIS) | The JICA-built customs clearance system, live since 2014. VNACCS = clearance, VCIS = intelligence/risk. **Declarants never touch it directly** — they use vendor middleware, dominantly **ECUS5-VNACCS (Thái Sơn)**. **No public data feed**; it is a declaration-processing system, not a data source. Scheduled for replacement by "Hải quan số" targeted 31/12/2026 (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 08, 10). |
| **Công báo** | The official gazette (`congbao.chinhphu.vn`) — the **publication of record**. Organized by gazette issue rather than document identity, and carries **no hiệu lực status and no relationship metadata** by design, so it must be paired with vbpl.vn rather than used alone. But it is `Allow: /`, needs no JS, ships **DOCX + text-layer PDF**, and its files are the `_signed` authoritative versions. **The irony to internalize: the two "convenient" aggregators (vbpl legacy paths, thuvienphapluat) are technically blocked and legally hostile, while the authoritative primary source is wide open and has strictly better data** (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 04, 12). |
| **Phân luồng** | Channel assignment after declaration: **green** = clear, **yellow** = document check, **red** = physical inspection. Driven by the risk-management system. A wrong HS history is a documented path to being flagged **luồng đỏ / high-risk** — a knock-on cost that dwarfs the fine (verified 2026-07-17, source: research reports 08, 09). |

## Technical Context

- **Stack:** NestJS + PostgreSQL (with pgvector) + Docker. Plain internal web app.
- **Why this stack and nothing more:** every excluded component in [Out of Scope](#out-of-scope) was excluded because v1's core is a keyed table read plus a bounded retrieval over HS notes. PostgreSQL does both. Each added service is operational surface a 5–50-person internal tool cannot amortize.
- **Data model non-negotiables:**
  - **Bitemporal from day 1.** Not retrofittable. See [In Scope](#in-scope).
  - **HS version is a dimension with validity dates**, not a constant. AHTN 2022 today; HS 2028 from 01/01/2028.
  - **Schedule/annex is part of the key.** The 94%-wrong parser is what happens when it isn't.
  - **Version efficiency trick worth stealing:** a new parent temporal version on date Dₙ is an *aggregation* that reuses the existing versions of unchanged children — you don't duplicate the whole Luật on every amendment (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00039). Caveat: that model implements **valid time only, not true bitemporality**, and **reports no quantitative evaluation** — adopt the data model, don't cite it as proof of performance.
- **Retrieval guidance for the HS-notes surface (and for later RAG):**
  - **Hybrid BM25 + dense, with the weighting a function of how well-adapted the embedding is.** Out-of-the-box dense embeddings *lose* to BM25 on Vietnamese legal text (SBV-LawGraph, ALQAC2025: BM25 R@1 **0.57** vs naive dense R@1 **0.36**; their hybrid weights **75% BM25 / 25% semantic**). Fine-tuned dense *annihilates* BM25 (TVPL corpus: BM25 MRR@10 **21.60** → fine-tuned ColBERT **74.61**, fine-tuned bi-encoder **70.69** — but only after fine-tuning on 507K synthetic queries). **Do not copy a weight ratio from a paper whose embedding model you aren't using** (verified 2026-07-17, sources: https://lexuanbach.github.io/publication/ACIIDS2026a.pdf, https://arxiv.org/html/2412.00657v1, https://arxiv.org/html/2409.13699v1).
  - **Why BM25 is structurally strong here:** terms of art and citation strings ("Điều 12", "Nghị định 168/2024/NĐ-CP") must match *exactly*, and dense embeddings blur precisely the tokens carrying decisive legal weight — the canonical example being `unverzüglich` vs `sofort`; the Vietnamese equivalents are **"có thể" vs "phải"**, "trong thời hạn" vs "chậm nhất".
  - **Document-Level Retrieval Mismatch (DRM)** — retrieving a right-looking chunk from entirely the wrong văn bản — was observed at **>95% on some datasets**. Prepending a ~150-char document-level summary to each chunk before embedding roughly **halved** it, and *generic* summaries beat expert-guided ones. Highly relevant to Vietnam, where "Điều 5. Giải thích từ ngữ" is near-textually identical across hundreds of văn bản. If you adopt summary prefixes, **exclude the prefix from the BM25 index** (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://arxiv.org/html/2510.06999v1).
  - **Vietnamese word segmentation is model-coupled, not a free choice.** PhoBERT-family models were pretrained on segmented text; you must segment at inference to match pretraining (RDRSegmenter/VnCoreNLP, Underthesea, PyVi are all in published use). The 256-token limit of PhoBERT-based models *forces* sub-Điều chunking (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 02).
  - **Keep retrieved context small.** Legal RAG is vulnerable to lost-in-the-middle; dumping 20 Điều into context actively degrades faithfulness. Argues for aggressive reranking and low top-k (k=5 in published practice), not "retrieve more to be safe."
- **Grounding — the guarantee that is worthless.** In the only preregistered evaluation of leading legal AI tools (202 queries, expert-scored), **Lexis+ AI: 65% accurate, hallucinates >17%; Westlaw AI-Assisted Research: 42% accurate, hallucinates >34%**, against vendor claims of "hallucination-free" (verified 2026-07-17, source: https://doi.org/10.1111/jels.12413). The decisive example: asked for opinions by a **fictitious judge**, Lexis+ AI returned a **real case with a real, correctly-formatted citation** — not written by that nonexistent judge. **"Every citation resolves to a real document" is the guarantee vendors market and exactly the one that fails.** Our grounding check must verify **the cited provision supports the proposition asserted**, not that it exists. A Điều-number validator gives false comfort. Refusal must be a first-class output.
- **Ingestion:** Công báo `.doc` as the sole tariff source; annex-aware parsing with a real Word parser; explicit `*` / TRQ / absolute-duty / C/O-conditional states; effective-date **and expiry**-aware temporal model rather than "latest".

## Constraints

**Inherited from APB** (see [Agent Rules](AGENTS.md)):
- **All project documentation, code, comments, identifiers, commit messages, filenames and API names in English.** The owner is Vietnamese; the repo rule is English docs.
- **Communicate with the project owner in Vietnamese.**
- **Commit only when requested.** Never start implementation before planning and design are reviewed.
- Plain Markdown with relative links; no Obsidian-only syntax; notes self-contained enough to read outside a graph view.

**Domain constraints — these are not negotiable and not deferrable:**
- **Bitemporal from day 1.** Retrofitting temporality onto a "latest value" schema is a rewrite, and until it exists every answer is silently possibly-wrong. The petrol case is the proof: ND 72/2026 was law on the day it was signed, machine-readable 15 days later, and **expired 52 days after that** — a "latest" design serves 0% forever.
- **Tariff numbers never via LLM.** Not for extraction into the store, not for transformation, not for presentation. The number is read from a keyed table or it is not shown.
- **HS never autonomous.** Candidates + verbatim evidence + a human decision, always. Abstention is a valid and often the best output.
- **Never launder an uncertain claim into a confident one.** Where sources conflict, show both. Where something is unverified, say so. Concealing conflict behind a confident single answer is the actively harmful design — and it is what the 76%-complaint environment punishes.
- **Cite the decree and the as-of date on every answer, and refuse when the snapshot may be stale.** This tool is a research aid that shows its sources. It is never an answer engine that states a rate.

## Open Questions

**Blocking — resolve before schema design:**

1. **Are vbpl's `provisionTree` and `referenceProvisions` ever populated?** Both were **`null` on every document sampled**. The Server Action JSON carries `references[] → {targetDocument{...}, referenceType:int, referenceProvisions}`, and `referenceProvisions` *implies* provision-level (điều/khoản) linking. If populated site-wide, that is a provision-level legal graph — and the April 2026 relaunch press claims exactly that (*"quản lý chi tiết đến từng điều, khoản, điểm... máy có thể tự động đọc, hiểu"*). **Research report 04 calls this "the highest-value open question."** Action: test 10–20 recent documents before designing the schema (source: research report 04).
2. **What are the routes on `vbpl-bientap-gateway.moj.gov.vn/api`?** The gateway was **found, is publicly reachable and unauthenticated at the edge** (extracted from JS chunk `3600-110a43f99391da6f.js`), but **could not be enumerated** — every probed path 404s, `/actuator` exposes only `health`, no Swagger, and the frontend calls it server-side via Next.js Server Actions so routes never appear client-side. **Found but unmapped.** A documented API would delete the entire headless-browser step. Report 04 estimates ~30 more minutes of work (source: research report 04).
3. **What is the `referenceType` int → label mapping?** Observed values `3`, `12` (and elsewhere a histogram `{4:1, 3:1, 12:2, 9:1}`). The **27 relation labels are known; the join is not** (source: research reports 04, 07).

**Unresolved contradiction — two research agents directly disagree:**

4. **Is the customs.gov.vn tariff API reachable, and is it captcha-gated?** **Both reports investigated; they contradict each other, and the conflict is unresolved. Present both; do not pick a winner without a fresh test.**
   - **Report 10 says: verified working, no captcha.** It drove the page in Chrome, captured the XHR, and reproduced it with bare `curl`: `POST https://www.customs.gov.vn/bridge?url=/customs/servletws/bieuthue/APIBieuThue`, body `{"l_class":"TIM_KIEM","l_action":"GET","l_param":"8703","l_bieu_thue":"NK_uu_dai"}`. Claims **no auth, no JSESSIONID, no captcha, no Referer/Origin check — the on-page captcha is client-side only and the API does not enforce it.** `l_param` = HS prefix, min 4 digits; `"8703"` returned **510 rows**, one row per HS line, one column per regime, with values consistent with 2026 phase-downs. Reports discovery calls returning **26 import schedule codes**. Estimates bulk extraction at **~1,228 POSTs**. (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 10)
   - **Report 12 says: could not reach it, and found a captcha endpoint.** `/scripts/main.js` hardcodes `http://123.30.210.236:8080/hqcustomsapi/` — a raw IP, plain HTTP, port 8080 — **including `.../hqcustomsapi/captcha/CheckCaptcha`**, so at least part of the portal is CAPTCHA-gated. **That IP timed out.** Report 12 explicitly declines to claim unreachability: it cannot distinguish geo-fencing from a sandbox egress block. It calls enumerating ~11k codes through a CAPTCHA-fronted undocumented endpoint on a raw IP "fragile and adversarial." (verified 2026-07-17, source: research report 12)
   - **Note the two may be describing different endpoints** (`customs.gov.vn/bridge?url=...` vs a raw-IP `hqcustomsapi` host) — that is a hypothesis, not a finding, and nobody tested it. **Both reports agree on the conclusion that matters: the API is a convenience layer with no ToS grant, no SLA, no versioning, and no legal authority. The legal source of truth is the decree text. v1 must not depend on it.**

**Non-blocking but open:**

5. **Does a proper Word parser fix the EVFTA rate-column collapse?** See [Unverified](#unverified--do-not-rely-on) — this is the one gap a builder must close before trusting any parsed table.
6. **Is the `customs.gov.vn` API's FTA coverage usable at all?** Report 10 itself flags: **no VIFTA and no CEPA entries**, `THOI_GIAN_CAP_NHAT` values of **2019–2020**, and **current-year rates only** — no forward-year series. Even on report 10's own account, 2027 rates and two FTAs must come from decree annexes.
7. Whether `APIBieuThue` rate-limits (report 10 did not probe aggressively). Whether ND 128/2022 is tariff-related at all. Sustained-rate crawl behavior on vbpl at 158k scale (~40 requests made; no throttling observed, which is not evidence of none). Công báo total corpus size and date coverage — never enumerated.

## Unverified / Do Not Rely On

Reproduced from the research's own flags. **Do not restate any of these as fact, and do not build against them without fresh confirmation.**

- **The EVFTA table-parse artifact — the one gap that must close first.** On the EVFTA gazette file, `textutil` collapsed a table row to `2101.11.11 | ...không dưới 20kg | 2925,421,818,114,510,9` — six rates (`29 | 25,4 | 21,8 | 18,1 | 14,5 | 10,9`) concatenated with **no delimiter**, in a decimal-comma locale. **Unrecoverable without heuristics.** Report 12 states plainly: *"I am inferring, not asserting, that this is a tooling artifact"* — RCEP has the identical 6-year structure and extracted perfectly, which strongly suggests a table-aware parser (LibreOffice → docx → `w:tbl/w:tr/w:tc`) fixes it, but **no `soffice`/`antiword`/`python-docx` was available to prove it.** *"This is the one gap a builder must close before trusting anything."*
